About Noobgramming Adventures

What on Earth is This?

Noobgramming Adventures is a personal project made by a noobgrammer. The goal is to learn how to code from scratch and share all the things this noobgrammer learns by making "fun" (hopefully) and informative content!

Who is behind this?

I'm not sure why you would ask but the man behind this is "Tres." Someone who is interested in learning how to write words that do stuffs. He also loves his stupid ideas. One of which is the creation of this thing you're seeing right now!

Why Are You Doing This?

I'm not trying to torture anyone by exposing the noob's programming perspective. I only do this for two reasons:
  1. - a refresher for me when I become less noob;
  2. - helping hand to other people who are in similar position to me;
  3. - to have fun learning!
(wait... that's more than two, isn't it?)
Anyway, doing simple monotoned tutorial or guides are not fun for me. I also don't want look like some of those "superior" content creators who act like they know everything and they're always right.
And that's when I thought of making this unusual content! The birth of the Noobgrammer and Noobgramming Adventures!
